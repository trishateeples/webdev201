Chinese Immersion Program!

Know before you join...

  1. Must start in first grade
  2. Half of the student's day is in Chinese
  3. Finish a few classes away from a Chinese Minor
    • 6 classes at BYU
    • 2 classes at Utah State
    • 2 classes at UVU
  4. Student's will learn a lot about Chinese culture
  5. Take Bridge courses through the UofU
