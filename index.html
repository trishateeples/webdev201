We are the Teeples family! Jill and Troy served their LDS mission in Italy. In 2012, We built a wood-fired pizza oven in our backyard. We have been making pizzas ever since!

  • Dough and sauce made from scratch
  • Base pizza is a margherita
  • Variety of toppings to choose

Once we became more comfortable with making and cooking pizzas, we started to have friends, family and neighbors over for "pizza parties". They all loved it and said they would totally buy our pizza. We thought about it and decided to start Teeps'a Pizza!

Why Choose Teeps'a Pizza?

We make delicious homemade pizzas!! They cook in less than 90 seconds. We love to get some of the toppings fresh from out garden.
